About Me

I have a lifelong passion for understanding and solving problems. Whether coding, simulations or puzzles, I am driven to dig in and understand what is going on and loves when the lightbulb goes on and I discover the solution or the missing piece. I love collaborating with others to watch ideas become reality. When I am interested in a subject I need to learn everything I can about it.

I have a Bachelor of Science degree in chemical engineering and 12+ years experience in designing small and large oil and gas facilities around the world. I am experienced in debugging simulation software and designing fit-for-purpose client solutions while collaborating with remote and diverse multi-disciplinary teams.

Because I understand the world is in the middle of a technological revolution, I was thrilled to complete the Full Stack Software Development program through Bow Valley College (https://bowvalleycollege.ca) in collaboration with EDGE UP (Energy to Digital Growth Education and Upskilling Project - https://edgeupyyc.com) , Calgary Economic Development (https://calgaryeconomicdevelopment.com) and ICTC (Information and Communication Technology Council - https://www.ictc-ctic.ca). I am excited and ready to offer my knowledge in web and software development utilizing HTML, CSS, JS, React, Node.js/Express, SQL Server, and C# to solving the next tech puzzle.

When not working, you can find me with my nose in a book, enjoying time with family and friends, testing out a new recipe or paddling on the reservoir.
